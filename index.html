Tlayudas




While I wouldn't say Tlayudas are my all-time favorite, it's only because I can't decide between them and entomatadas. Despite that, Tlayudas are amazing and one of my favorite foods to eat. They have everything I love: tortilla, chorizo, steak, beans, tomatoes, and cheese.

Tlayudas are a delightful mix of textures and flavors. The crispy tortilla serves as the perfect base for the savory chorizo and steak, while the beans add a creamy element. The fresh tomatoes and melted cheese on top bring it all together in a harmonious, mouthwatering dish.

I have some great memories eating this, but my jaw always hurts at the end because of how much it is and how chewy it is. The first bite is always the best one, and I'm so glad to be Hispanic and was exposed to this food at a young age.
